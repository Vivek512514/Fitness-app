Exercise Technique

Squats

· Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out
· Chest up, core tight throughout
· Thighs parallel to floor at the bottom
· Drive through heels to stand
💡 Biggest muscle group → most testosterone → fat loss

Push-ups

· Hands slightly wider than shoulders
· Body in a straight plank line — no sagging hips
· Lower chest to ~1 inch from floor
· Can't do full ones? Start on knees — completely fine
💡 Directly targets chest. Muscle shows as fat reduces

Dumbbell Rows

· One hand/knee on bench, back flat
· Pull dumbbell to hip, squeeze at top
· Don't rotate your torso
💡 Back strength + posture makes chest look flatter immediately

Plank

· Forearms on floor, body straight
· Squeeze abs and glutes — breathe normally
· Start 20 sec if needed, build from there
💡 Core stability protects your lower back in all other exercises
Diet Reminders
· Protein first at every meal — eggs, paneer, dal, chicken, curd
· Reduce white rice portions; fill rest with vegetables
· Eat at regular times — skipping then binging worsens insulin resistance
· Water: minimum 2.5–3 litres daily
💡 80% consistency for months beats 100% for 2 weeks then quitting
Bloodwork — Do This Soon
Important: Your chest fat + fatigue may have a hormonal cause. Exercise alone won't fully fix it without this.
· Total Testosterone + Free Testosterone
· Estradiol (E2)
· TSH, T3, T4 — thyroid panel
· Fasting insulin + glucose
· LH, FSH (if testosterone comes back low)
